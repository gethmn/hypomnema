# Country sourdough

A weekend sourdough loaf leavened entirely with a mature starter — no
commercial yeast.

## Schedule

Feed the starter the night before so it triples by morning. Mix the
final dough at 8 a.m. for a bake around 4 p.m.

## Method

Combine 100 g active starter with 350 g water and stir until milky.
Add 500 g of bread flour and 10 g of salt; mix to a shaggy mass and
rest 45 minutes for autolyse.

Bulk-ferment at 26 °C with four sets of stretch-and-folds at
30-minute intervals during the first two hours, then leave undisturbed
until the dough has expanded by roughly 50 percent — typically four to
six hours total at room temperature.

Pre-shape into a round, bench-rest 20 minutes, then shape tightly and
proof seam-up in a banneton for 90 minutes. Score and bake covered at
240 °C for 20 minutes; uncover and bake another 20 minutes at 220 °C.
